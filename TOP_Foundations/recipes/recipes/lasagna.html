Lasagna recipe

This lasagna takes a little bit work but it is so satisfying and filling thats it's worth it!

Ingridients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
